# Online Payment Options Comparison - Morocco

## For High-Ticket Furniture (600€ - 6000€+)

### 1. Stripe
**Best for:** International cards, high-ticket items, modern UX

**Pros:**
- ✅ Excellent for high-ticket orders
- ✅ Supports Moroccan Dirham (MAD)
- ✅ Strong fraud protection
- ✅ Modern checkout experience
- ✅ Webhook support for order sync
- ✅ Good documentation

**Cons:**
- ❌ Higher fees (2.9% + 0.30€ per transaction)
- ❌ Requires business verification for Morocco
- ❌ May have payout delays

**Fees:**
- 2.9% + 0.30€ per transaction
- No monthly fee
- Chargeback fee: 15€

**Support:**
- Excellent (email, chat, phone)
- Available in English and French

**Risk Level:** Low (strong fraud protection)

**Recommendation:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **BEST CHOICE** for high-ticket items

---

### 2. PayPal
**Best for:** International customers, trust factor

**Pros:**
- ✅ Widely recognized and trusted
- ✅ Supports Morocco
- ✅ Buyer protection
- ✅ Easy integration

**Cons:**
- ❌ Higher fees (3.4% + fixed fee)
- ❌ Can hold funds for high-ticket items
- ❌ Less control over checkout flow
- ❌ Account can be frozen

**Fees:**
- 3.4% + 0.35€ per transaction (domestic)
- 3.9% + fixed fee (international)
- No monthly fee

**Support:**
- Good (email, phone)
- Available in multiple languages

**Risk Level:** Medium (account holds possible)

**Recommendation:** ⭐⭐⭐ Good backup option

---

### 3. Paylink (Local Moroccan)
**Best for:** Local customers, lower fees

**Pros:**
- ✅ Lower fees for local transactions
- ✅ Supports Moroccan banks
- ✅ Local support
- ✅ Good for Moroccan market

**Cons:**
- ❌ Less known internationally
- ❌ Limited documentation
- ❌ May not support all card types
- ❌ Integration complexity

**Fees:**
- ~2.5% per transaction (varies)
- Monthly fee possible

**Support:**
- Good (local support in French/Arabic)

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Recommendation:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good for local market

---

### 4. CMI (Cash Management International)
**Best for:** Moroccan market, bank integration

**Pros:**
- ✅ Strong in Morocco
- ✅ Bank integration
- ✅ Local support

**Cons:**
- ❌ Less modern UX
- ❌ Limited international support
- ❌ Integration complexity

**Fees:**
- ~2.5-3% per transaction

**Support:**
- Good (local support)

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Recommendation:** ⭐⭐⭐ Consider for local focus

---

## Recommendation for BK Agencements

### Primary: Stripe
**Why:**
1. Best for high-ticket items (600€-6000€+)
2. Excellent fraud protection
3. Modern checkout experience
4. Strong webhook support
5. Good documentation

**Setup:**
- Register business account
- Verify business in Morocco
- Enable Moroccan Dirham
- Set up webhooks

### Secondary: Bank Transfer
**Why:**
1. No fees
2. Trusted by high-ticket customers
3. Common in Morocco for large purchases
4. Manual verification process

### Tertiary: Cash on Delivery (COD)
**Why:**
1. Common in Morocco
2. No payment processing fees
3. Trust factor
4. Requires fraud checks

---

## Implementation Strategy

1. **Start with Stripe** for online payments
2. **Add Bank Transfer** as alternative
3. **Keep COD** for local deliveries
4. **Add PayPal** later if needed for international customers
